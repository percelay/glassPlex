[About Us]
Glasplex Plastics LLC operates a full plastic fabrication facility specializing in Acrylic, Lexan, Polycarbonate, and various Industrial Plastic Sheets. We provide custom fabrication and sales of plastic sheets in standard, custom-cut, and oversized dimensions. Our clientele includes major brands like QVC and News 12 New Jersey. We sell Business-to-Business (B2B) and directly to the public.
